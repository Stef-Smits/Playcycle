Modular Gaming Controller

Upgrade parts, not your whole controller

Playcycle is a modular, repairable controller for gamers tired of stick drift, broken buttons, and plastic waste. Swap modules, fix issues fast, and keep playing longer.

Join the Early Access Squad

Built for Gamers, Not Landfills

We design Playcycle with real gamers in mind. Here’s how we improve the gaming experience:

  • Less rage, more game: Replace only the broken module instead of buying a new controller.
  • Level up, don’t start over: Upgrade sticks, grips, or buttons as your playstyle evolves.
  • Reduce e-waste: Keep one controller for years, replacing only what wears out.
